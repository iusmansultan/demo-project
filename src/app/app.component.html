Meet Transroad

Engineered and implemented a software solution for fleet management

Visual Language
for Mobile App

The visual language developed for Transroad is based on a clean and minimalistic design, with a focus on typography, color, and imagery.

Driver
Application

The app is designed to provide a simple and effective way for drivers to connect with fleets that are looking for their services. It can be accessed easily via mobile devices.

Customer
Application

The application allows customers to create a profile and manage all their shipping needs in one place. Customers can request quotes, book shipments, and track their cargo from pickup to delivery.
